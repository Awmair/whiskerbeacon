Questions?

The things every owner asks first.

Do I have to switch my practice management software?
No, and that's the whole point. WhiskerBeacon sits on top of the tools you already run, whether that's Avimark, Cornerstone, ezyVet, Vetspire or something else. We wire what you have into one system. Nothing gets ripped out.
Is WhiskerFetch actually free, or is it a trial?
Genuinely free, and yours to keep. You pick one workflow, we build it fully functional, and it stays working whether or not you ever buy anything else. No expiry, no crippled features, no card required.
Will AI be making medical decisions about my patients?
No. We stay strictly in the front office and back office: booking, reminders, follow-ups, recall, charge checks. Diagnosis, dosing and every clinical call stays with your team. We won't go near the exam room.
How long does a build take?
A single WhiskerFetch workflow lands in days. The full WhiskerPack is a short, fixed-scope build, not a months-long project. You'll see something working quickly, because the work itself is the proof.
What does it actually cost?
WhiskerFetch is free. WhiskerPack starts around $2,000 to build plus $400 to $700 a month to keep running. WhiskerShadow, for multi-site or complex setups, starts from $6,000 plus monthly. Against a clinic's monthly revenue, the build pays back fast when it recovers even a handful of lapsed patients.
