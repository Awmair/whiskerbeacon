Questions

What partners ask before signing on

Is this the same as SEO?+
Different channel, some overlap. SEO gets your pages ranked in Google's link list. GEO gets your firm named inside the AI-generated answer — which is what potential clients see first. Research shows only 45% overlap between firms that rank well on Google and firms that appear in AI answers. You need both.
How many clients actually use AI to find lawyers?+
AI usage for local search jumped from 6% to 45% in a single year. For legal specifically, 13% of people who needed legal help used an AI chatbot in their research — up from near zero in 2023. That number is accelerating. The firms that establish AI visibility now lock in an advantage that gets harder to dislodge as competition catches on.
Do you work with competing firms in the same city?+
No. We take one PI firm per major metro. The first firm to engage us in a given market gets exclusivity. First-mover advantage is real in AI search, and we protect it for our clients.
What's in the free check?+
We run 10 real PI queries in your city and practice area across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini. You get a one-page report: which firms AI names, where competitors have coverage you don't, and one concrete fix. Delivered in 48 hours. No call required to receive it.
How long before we see results?+
Technical fixes from the Foundation Sprint show impact within 3–5 weeks as AI crawlers re-index. Content authority builds over 60–90 days. We set honest baselines on day one and send a monthly re-test report so you see the exact movement — not just our word for it.
What does this cost?+
The visibility check is free. The Foundation Sprint starts at $1,750 (up to $3,500 for larger multi-practice firms). The monthly retainer starts at $597 and scales to $5,500/month for multi-city firms. One PI case with an average settlement covers years of this service. No lock-in on the retainer.
