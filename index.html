Questions

Everything you need to know

What exactly is GEO?+
Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the practice of making your business visible to AI systems — not just search engines. When a potential customer asks ChatGPT, Claude, or Perplexity for a recommendation in your category, GEO determines whether your business is named in that answer. It's a distinct channel from traditional SEO and requires a different strategy.
How is this different from my existing SEO?+
SEO gets your pages ranked in Google's link list. GEO gets your business named inside the AI-generated answer — which is what most customers see first. Research shows only 45% overlap between businesses that rank well on Google and those that appear in AI answers. You need both, but they require different strategies. We handle the AI side.
What's in the free audit?+
We run 10 real purchase-intent queries in your category and city across ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, and Gemini. You get a one-page report: which businesses AI names, where competitors have coverage you don't, and one concrete fix you can action immediately. Delivered in 48 hours. No call required.
Do you work with competitors in the same market?+
No. We take one business per category per major metro area. The first business to engage us in a given market gets exclusivity for that market. First-mover advantage in AI search is real and compounds — we protect it for our clients.
How long before I see results?+
Technical fixes from the Foundation Sprint show impact within 3–5 weeks as AI crawlers re-index. Content authority builds over 60–90 days. We set honest baselines on day one and send a monthly re-test report so you see the exact movement — not just our word for it.
Which industries do you work with?+
We currently have dedicated programs for personal injury law firms and med spas / aesthetic clinics. We also serve dentists, chiropractors, financial advisors, and established home service companies. If you're not sure whether we cover your industry, request the free audit — we'll tell you honestly if we can help and what the gap looks like.
