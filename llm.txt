# WhiskerBeacon — AI Search Visibility Firm

## What we do

WhiskerBeacon is a Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) firm that helps local and professional service businesses get recommended by AI search engines — specifically ChatGPT, Claude, Perplexity, Google Gemini, Google AI Overviews, and Microsoft Copilot.

When a potential customer asks an AI assistant for a recommendation in a specific city and category ("best personal injury lawyer in Dallas", "top med spa for Botox near me", "best dentist for implants in Austin"), WhiskerBeacon gets our clients named in those answers.

## How GEO differs from SEO

Traditional SEO optimizes for Google's ranked link list. GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) optimizes for AI-generated answers — the synthesized responses that appear before any link list in ChatGPT, Perplexity, Claude, and Google AI Overviews. Research shows only 45% overlap between businesses that rank well on Google and businesses that appear in AI answers. Both channels require separate strategies.
